<idea>
  **Design Movement**: Industrial Brutalism meets Cyberpunk Minimalism
  **Core Principles**:
  1. **Raw Functionality**: Expose the structure, prioritize data over decoration.
  2. **High Contrast & Precision**: Use sharp contrasts to guide focus, mimicking industrial precision instruments.
  3. **Immediacy**: Information should be instantly parseable, like a cockpit display.
  4. **Tactile Feedback**: Interactions should feel mechanical and responsive.

  **Color Philosophy**:
  - **Backgrounds**: Deep, almost black greys (Onyx, Charcoal) to reduce eye strain in low-light industrial environments.
  - **Accents**: Electric Blue (Cyan) for active states and data points, signaling "system active". Alert Orange for warnings.
  - **Intent**: Evoke a sense of robust, professional reliability. The dark mode isn't just aesthetic; it's practical for battery saving and focus.

  **Layout Paradigm**:
  - **Modular Dashboard**: A bento-box style grid for the dashboard, but with rigid, visible borders.
  - **Split-Screen Workflow**: For desktop, list on left, details on right. For mobile, stackable cards that expand.
  - **No "Floating" Elements**: Everything is anchored. No drop shadows for depth, but rather borders and background shades.

  **Signature Elements**:
  - **Monospace Typography for Data**: Use a high-quality monospace font (like JetBrains Mono or Geist Mono) for all IDs, timestamps, and metrics.
  - **Visible Grid Lines**: Subtle, thin lines separating sections, reinforcing the "blueprint" feel.
  - **Status Indicators**: Small, glowing dots or bars that mimic physical LEDs.

  **Interaction Philosophy**:
  - **Click = Action**: Buttons should have a "pressed" state that feels substantial.
  - **Swipe Actions**: On mobile, swipe to register quick actions (like "Check-in").
  - **Instant Feedback**: Every action triggers a micro-animation (e.g., a border flash) to confirm input.

  **Animation**:
  - **Mechanical Transitions**: Slides and reveals should be snappy (ease-out-quart), not floaty.
  - **Data Loading**: Instead of spinners, use a "scanning" line or a blinking cursor effect.

  **Typography System**:
  - **Headings**: Inter (Tight tracking, uppercase) for a commanding presence.
  - **Body**: Inter (Regular) for readability.
  - **Data/Code**: JetBrains Mono (for that technical, engineering look).
</idea>

  **Design Movement**: Swiss Style (International Typographic Style) - Dark Mode Edition
  **Core Principles**:
  1. **Grid Systems**: Absolute adherence to a mathematical grid.
  2. **Asymmetry**: Dynamic layouts that balance whitespace with content.
  3. **Sans-Serif Purity**: Typography is the primary design element.
  4. **Objective Photography**: Use real, unadorned photos of machinery/assets.

  **Color Philosophy**:
  - **Backgrounds**: Matte Black (#111111) and Dark Grey (#222222).
  - **Accents**: Swiss Red (#FF3B30) for critical actions, Pure White for text.
  - **Intent**: Clarity and objectivity. The design recedes to let the content (the operational data) stand out.

  **Layout Paradigm**:
  - **Typographic Hierarchy**: Large, bold headings contrast with small, precise body text.
  - **Whitespace as Structure**: Use negative space to group related information, rather than borders or boxes.
  - **Left-Aligned Everything**: Strong vertical axis.

  **Signature Elements**:
  - **Huge Typography**: Page titles and key metrics are massive.
  - **Geometric Shapes**: Simple circles or squares for icons and indicators.
  - **Horizontal Rules**: Thick lines to separate major sections.

  **Interaction Philosophy**:
  - **Hover Reveals**: Information is progressively disclosed on hover.
  - **Smooth Scrolling**: A sense of continuity.

  **Animation**:
  - **Parallax**: Subtle movement of background elements relative to foreground.
  - **Fade-ins**: Content gently fades in as it loads.

  **Typography System**:
  - **Primary**: Helvetica Now Display (or a close open-source alternative like Ungeometria or Work Sans) - Bold, tight.
  - **Secondary**: Roboto or Arial - Neutral, legible.

<idea>
  **Design Movement**: Glassmorphism / HUD (Heads-Up Display)
  **Core Principles**:
  1. **Translucency**: Layers of information with blurred backgrounds.
  2. **Depth via Blur**: Hierarchy established by how "close" the layer feels.
  3. **Neon Glows**: Subtle outer glows to highlight active elements.
  4. **Futuristic Utility**: Feels like a tool from a sci-fi movie.

  **Color Philosophy**:
  - **Backgrounds**: Deep Navy or Midnight Blue gradients.
  - **Accents**: Neon Green and Hot Pink gradients for buttons and graphs.
  - **Intent**: To make the operator feel like they are using cutting-edge tech. High engagement.

  **Layout Paradigm**:
  - **Floating Cards**: Content sits on semi-transparent cards floating above a dark, abstract background.
  - **Central Focus**: Key data is centered, with controls on the periphery.

  **Signature Elements**:
  - **Frosted Glass**: Background blur (backdrop-filter: blur) on all panels.
  - **Thin Borders**: 1px semi-transparent white borders to define edges.
  - **Gradient Text**: For headers and key metrics.

  **Interaction Philosophy**:
  - **Fluidity**: Elements morph and change shape.
  - **Glow on Hover**: Elements light up when interacted with.

  **Animation**:
  - **Slow Drifts**: Background elements move slowly.
  - **Soft Zooms**: Modals zoom in gently.

  **Typography System**:
  - **Primary**: Rajdhani or Orbitron (Tech/Sci-fi feel).
  - **Body**: Quicksand or Montserrat (Rounded, friendly).
</idea>
